Homework 8: Geoprocessing Analysis

David Gray

For this homework I wanted to determine which areas of land within the triangle area (Raleigh, Durham, Chapel-Hill) were located within 1 mile of a fire station. In order to accomplish this, I imported a geojson of all fire stations in NC and then used a buffer with a radius of 1-mile for each of the fire stations. I then imported a geojson of Census Urban Areas and focused my map on the triangle area. I then used an intersection that intersected the buffer fire stations with the urban areas in the triangle. When the map was completed I was able to see which areas had a large concetration of fire stations such as Central Raleigh. Other areas to the North and South of Raleigh had places where you could potentially live a few miles from the nearest fire station. The implications of this analysis and map could be that if you are deciding between places to live in this area you may consider living in an area that is closer to a fire station rather than one that is not close to any.
